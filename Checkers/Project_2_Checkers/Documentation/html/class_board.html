Moves a piece on our board.

Parameters
ourPieceOur piece.
captPcesA map to keep count of captured pieces.
treeA tree to hold the pieces a player has captured.

Moves a piece on our board. We pass in our piece (Red/Black). We need to pass our piece in, so that we can ensure we only choose to move a piece that's ours. We also pass in a map that will be used to keep count of how many pieces we've captured. The key for the map will be our piece, and the value will be how many pieces we've captured. Lastly, we pass in a tree that will hold the pieces we've captured.
